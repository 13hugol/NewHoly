About New Holy Cross School

New Holy Cross School, located in Kageshwori Manohara-09, Kathmandu, Nepal, has been Educating Young Minds for 25 Years. Our school is dedicated to fostering practical, experiential learning within a nurturing community environment. We invite you to join us on our journey of excellence and holistic development.

Our primary public presence is our Facebook page, where we share updates, event announcements, and school life. Please note: we do not currently have an official website or direct phone/email contact listed publicly; all inquiries are best made via Facebook Messenger.
