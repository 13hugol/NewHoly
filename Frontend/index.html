Message from the Principal

Mr. Dinesh Kumar Karki

Principal, New Holy Cross School

Welcome to New Holy Cross School! We are dedicated to "Educating Young Minds" and fostering a nurturing environment that promotes holistic development and excellence. Our journey of 25 years has been marked by a commitment to innovation, compassion, and curiosity, preparing students to thrive in an ever-changing world.

About New Holy Cross School

Our History & Philosophy

Founded 25 years ago, New Holy Cross School, located in Kageshwori Manohara-09, Kathmandu, has been a beacon of learning, dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity, personal growth, and a strong sense of community. Our philosophy centers on providing a holistic education that balances academic rigor with character development.

We believe in nurturing every student's unique potential, encouraging them to explore their interests, challenge themselves, and become responsible global citizens.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a dynamic world.

Vision: To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence and a nurturing environment that inspires lifelong learning.
