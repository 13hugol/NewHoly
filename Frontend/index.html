Welcome to New Holy Cross School! We are dedicated to "Educating Young Minds" and fostering a nurturing environment that promotes holistic development and excellence. Our journey of 25 years has been marked by a commitment to innovation, compassion, and curiosity, preparing students to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Through close collaboration with our dedicated staff, supportive parents, and the wider community, we aim to inspire lifelong learners and future leaders. Join us as we continue this journey of excellence together.

About New Holy Cross School

Our History & Philosophy

Founded 25 years ago, New Holy Cross School, located in Kageshwori Manohara-09, Kathmandu, has been a beacon of learning, dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity, personal growth, and a strong sense of community. Our philosophy centers on providing a holistic education that balances academic rigor with character development.

We believe in nurturing every student's unique potential, encouraging them to explore their interests, challenge themselves, and become responsible global citizens. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our innovative curriculum, dedicated faculty, and vibrant campus life.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a dynamic world.

Vision: To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching, and a nurturing environment that inspires lifelong learning.

Academic Programs

Our comprehensive curriculum emphasizes practical, experiential learning, designed to challenge and inspire students across all disciplines, preparing them for success in higher education and beyond.

Student Life at New Holy Cross

At New Holy Cross School, student life extends far beyond the classroom. We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities, clubs, and events designed to enrich our students' experiences, foster their talents, and build strong character.

From competitive sports teams and vibrant arts programs to academic clubs and community service initiatives, there's something for everyone. We encourage students to explore new interests, develop leadership skills, and forge lasting friendships in a supportive and engaging environment.
